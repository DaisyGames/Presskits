Description

SokoChess White is a puzzle game about pushing Chess pieces into their destination and overcoming various obstacles. Enjoy 70 different handcrafted levels with a unique twist on the Chess formula.

SokoChess White is a spin-off game based on the original SokoChess. This title removes the black pieces that will try to capture your pieces and instead introduces even more elements that go beyond chess.

Features

  • 70 levels
  • Infinite undos
  • Don't like a level you are playing? Skip it
  • Puzzle elements you won't see in Chess
  • Unique gameplay combining Chess and Sokoban
